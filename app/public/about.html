Who are we?


From personal experience at Drexel University, we have dealt with the challenges of finding subletters for Co-op relocations with limited resources. Lease-It! streamlines this process, making it easier for students to connect and secure housing efficiently.

Whether you're moving temporarily for a Co-op or transitioning between leases, our platform helps students find reliable sublets with ease. At Lease-It! we create a more seamless and stress-free leasing experience.
